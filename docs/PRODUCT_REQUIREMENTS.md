# Product Requirements

## Product position

EasyRemote is a cross-platform authorized device management and connection launcher.

The product goal is to provide a unified, low-cost interface for a user's own devices or devices they are explicitly authorized to manage.

## MVP goals

1. Device list and card-based home page.
2. Manual device records.
3. Route abstraction.
4. Protocol entry abstraction.
5. Adapter metadata registry.
6. WebDAV sync design.
7. Sensitive-data vault design.
8. Windows and Android first.
9. Future Linux, iOS, and HarmonyOS planning.

## Out of scope for this scaffold

- Working remote-control engine.
- Hidden access.
- Credential bypass.
- Any behavior that operates without explicit authorization.
